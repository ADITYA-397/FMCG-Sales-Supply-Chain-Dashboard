# FMCG-Sales-Supply-Chain-Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing 190,000+ FMCG transactions (2022-2024).
 
## What It Shows
 
**3 Dashboards:**
1. Sales Performance - Revenue, channels, promos
2. Supply Chain - Delivery, inventory, fulfillment
3. Forecasting - Demand prediction, growth trends
 
## Key Numbers
 
- Revenue: ₹59.70M
- Units Sold: 11M
- Fulfillment: 94.44%
- Delivery: 3 days avg
- Forecast Accuracy: 99.64%
 
## Data
 
- File: FMCG_2022_2024.csv (from Kaggle)
- Rows: 190,759 transactions
- Period: Jan 2022 - Dec 2024
- Categories: Yogurt, Milk, ReadyMeal, SnackBar, Juice
- Channels: Retail, E-commerce, Discount
 
## How to Use
 
1. Download `FMCG_Sales_Dashboard.pbix`
2. Open in Power BI Desktop
3. Use filters: Date, Category, Channel, Region
4. Explore charts and KPIs
 
## Key Insights
 
- Retail: 33.5% revenue share
- Promos drive 5.6x revenue lift
- Yogurt: 38% of total demand
- Growth rate: 78.84% YoY
